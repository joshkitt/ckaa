According to a recent whitepaper from the American Institute of Architects (AIA), buildings account for half of all greenhouse gas emissions. The building sector is the key source of demand for energy and materials that produce by-product greenhouse gases.

Sustainable design is becoming more and more important. Our clients understand this importance. Most green buildings are high-quality buildings; they last longer, cost less to operate and maintain, and provide greater occupant satisfaction than standard development. The Federal, State and local government bodies have enacted policies and incentives for renewable energy systems as well. Financial incentives are available for sustainable building in Utah.
